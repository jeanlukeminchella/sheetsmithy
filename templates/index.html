Well, you're in the right place! Sheet Smithy can help you create a ready-to-play character sheet in just a few seconds.

Ask your Game Master what level you're playing at, and then pick some options that sound cool (avoid a Cleric or Druid as they can be quite complicated). Choose a name that fits your character and is easy to pronounce using this website for inspiration. Once you have generated a character, bookmark it or print it off and give it a read. Knowing your class, species, and what dice you will need will be helpful before you start playing.

When it is your turn in a fight you get an Action, a Bonus Action and you can move up to your Speed. All our character sheets have most of the Action options you can do listed in the middle of the page and your Bonus Actions (if you have any) on the bottom right. If you see some "O"s following an ability, this means you can only use it so many times before running out. Be sure to cross off an "O" when you use it with a pencil, so you can regain the use when you take a rest. Some abilities may cost Spell Slots or other resources, which will need crossing off elsewhere on the sheet.

If an ability can be used from a distance, it will have its range in the title eg. Shortbow (80ft).

If you're not sure about anything drop me an email or ask your Game Master :) Good luck!
