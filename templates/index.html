Well, you're in the right place! Once you have generated a character, save it or print it off and give it a read. It may not make much sense until you have played, but having an idea about what dice you will need and what some Actions your character has will be helpful to know if they get in a fight.

When it is your turn in a game you get an Action, a Bonus Action and you can move up to your Speed. For your convinience, all our character sheets have most of the Action options you can do listed in the middle of the page and your Bonus Actions (if you have any) on the bottom right.

If you see some "o"s following an ability, this means you can only use it so many times before running out. Be sure to cross off an "o" when you use it with a pencil, so you can regain the use when you take a rest.

If you play a spellcaster, some abilities may cost Spell Slots or other resources, which will also need crossing off in the Spellcasting section. If it is your first time playing, I would recommend not playing a Cleric or Druid as they can be quite complicated.

If you're not sure about anything drop me an email or ask your Game Master :) Good luck!
